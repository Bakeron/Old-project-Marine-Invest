MARINE INVEST GROUP has been created by three companies that provides services in marine industry worldwide having their own facilities in Europe (Poland, Germany, Netherlands, Belgium, UK, Spain, Greece) and Asia (Shanghai, Singapore, Hong Kong, Taiwan).

During 10 years of our existence in marine industry companies from Marine Invest Group performed more than 100 services for over 100 customers every year. We are happy to provide our services and get back 100% satisfaction from our customers during 1000+ services since 2007.

Our History

  • Bartosz Jarmoliński starts a company that operates on shipyards, mainly as an work agency company which offers manpower to subcontractors.
  • German owners starts ordering manpower from Poland. Company is growing. Over 25 vessels has been serviced by company between 2009 and 2011.
  • Company focuses on instrument servicing, refrigeration services and supply of manpower. RAE & BW trainings are being taken in Netherlands.
  • Marine Invest Poland takes part in Dräger & MSA service trainings in UK. More refrigeration engineers are joining company’s refrigeration department.
  • RKI Analytical (Riken Keiki) grants authorization for both service and sales in area of Poland to Marine Invest as exclusive distributor and service provider.
  • Long awaited authorization from TQ Environmental company is given to Marine Invest Poland. Worldwide team grows to over 10 people.
  • Due to new regulations (F-gas) refrigeration engineers obtains proper certificates and as of year 2015 Marine Invest Poland is F-gas certified company.
  • Three companies of Marine Invest Group are established Marine Invest Poland, Marine Invest Refrigeration and Marine Invest Hydraulics.
  • Marine Invest Group company Marine Invest Hydraulics obtains DNV-GL approval of manufacturer certificate. Worldwide team grows to over 20 people.
